NoiseScript A script which uses a variety of algorithms; Perlin noise, Worley noise and fractal noise, to procedurally generate textures and images. Light2D A script that traces rays of light from a central point, stopping when it intersects walls. The user can draw in walls and move the point of light around the scene, with a Doomslayer type view on the right side.
