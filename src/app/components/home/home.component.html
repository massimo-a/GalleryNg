Massimo's Angular Gallery

NoiseScript A script which uses a variety of algorithms; Perlin noise, Worley noise and fractal noise, to procedurally generate textures and images. Light2D A script that traces rays of light from a central point, stopping when it intersects walls. The user can draw in walls and move the point of light around the scene, with a Doomslayer type view on the right side.
Work in progress Boids Boids is an artificial life program created by Craig Reynolds, which simulates the flocking behaviour of birds.
Conway's Game of Life An implementation of Conway's Game of Life, who first specified the rules for this Turing complete cellular automata Fluid Simulation A fluid simulation based on the paper Real-Time Fluid Dynamics for Games by Jon Stam and Fluid Simulation for Dummies by Mike Ash. Reaction Diffusion A simulation of a reaction between two chemicals, A and B. A certain quantity of A is added into the mix at each time step and a certain quantity of B is removed. B reacts with A, causing chemical A to become chemical B. The chemicals diffuse similar to how fluids diffuse. Produces interesting patterns and effects.
Diffusion Limited Aggregation An algorithm in which particles undergo a random walk, eventually colliding with an "aggregate". The aggregate becomes larger and larger, forming a tree or plant like structure.
